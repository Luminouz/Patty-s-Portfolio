baan

branding & UX/UI design

For my very first full-service design project I was hired to rebrand Baan, a San Francisco Thai restaurant. The word “Baan” means “home” in Thai and so I chose a concept that captured that meaning. I wanted a design that felt cozy and warm while simultaneously communicating a modern, contemporary, feel. As a part of the rebrand I was tasked with creating two versions of the visual identity: one for business, and one for personal use. The client requested the personal identity have a more feminine touch. My solution was to incorporate the client’s favorite flower (a lilac) into the final design. I collaborated with another designer on the website, and we agreed that using SquareSpace was the best solution for this project. We wanted a site that allowed the client to update the content at their leisure. We chose a template/typeface that matched our rebrand and made sure the user experience was a positive one. Finally, we staged a shoot of the restaurant to highlight its ambiance and food.
